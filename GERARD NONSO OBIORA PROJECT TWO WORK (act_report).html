Wangling and Analyze Data¶

Introduction¶

In this project, the dataset 'WeRateDogs' will be wrangled (and as well analyzed and visualized). WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog. These ratings almost always have a denominator of 10. The account has over four (4) million followers and has received international media coverage. Because real-world data rarely comes clean, data will thus be gathered (based on WeRateDogs) from a variety of sources with the end goal of analyzing and visualizing the collated information afterwards.

The Wrangling will follow this format:

  1. Data Gathering
  2. Assessing the Data
  3. Cleaning the Data

The jupyter notebook [GERARD NONSO OBIORA PROJECT TWO WORK (wrangle_act)] has the project code and the aim here is to communicate the insights and show the visualizations produced from my wrangled data.
